Grape Fruit Soap

₱234

Product Details

Our Grapefruit Soap is handcrafted using the traditional cold-process method, combining carefully measured olive oil, grapefruit essence, and caustic soda to produce a mild yet effective cleansing bar. During the soap-making process, the caustic soda fully reacts with the oils, ensuring no active lye remains in the final product. The result is a clean, refreshing soap with a crisp grapefruit aroma that provides an energizing, uplifting bathing experience. This soap gently cleanses the skin while leaving it feeling fresh, soft, and naturally revitalized.
